Taptly Child Safety Standards

Taptly is a contact exchange application designed for intentional in-person sharing of contact information.

No public discovery

The app does not allow browsing or searching for other users.

No messaging or social feed

Users cannot communicate with strangers inside the app.

Intentional exchange only

Information is shared only when two users deliberately scan a QR code or token in physical proximity.

No location sharing

The app does not provide live location or proximity tracking.

No minors targeting

Taptly is not directed to children and requires users to be at least 16 years old.

Reporting concerns

If you believe the app is being misused, contact: info@dotrem.gr

Taptly complies with child safety and anti-exploitation regulations and does not allow user discovery, anonymous interaction, or unsolicited contact.
