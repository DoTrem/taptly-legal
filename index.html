Privacy Policy – Taptly

Last updated: 09/02/2026

Taptly is a contact-sharing application that allows users to exchange selected personal information with other users through QR codes or tokens. By using the application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that the user explicitly provides inside the application profile.

This may include:
    •
  • Name or nickname
    • •
  • Email address
    • •
  • Phone number
    • •
  • Social media links
    • •
  • Profile image
    • •
  • Any optional fields the user chooses to store
We do not collect contacts from the device, call logs, SMS, or background device data.

2. How Information Is Used

Your information is used exclusively to provide the core functionality of the app:

• Display your profile • Share selected fields with another user when you explicitly initiate sharing • Allow another user to receive the shared data No information is shared automatically or without user action.

3. Data Sharing

Data is shared only when:

  • You press the share button
  • You select the specific fields to share
  • Another user scans your QR code or enters your token
The recipient receives only the fields you selected. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Data is securely stored using Supabase cloud infrastructure.

Security measures include:

• Authentication required for access • Row Level Security policies • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)

Temporary exchange sessions automatically expire after a short period.

5. Camera Permission

The camera is used only to scan QR codes for receiving shared information.

The app does not record video, take photos, or store camera content.

6. User Control

You can at any time:

• Edit your information • Delete fields • Delete your account (which removes your stored data)

7. Children’s Privacy

Taptly is not intended for children under 18 years old.

We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
